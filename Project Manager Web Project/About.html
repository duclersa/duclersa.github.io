Background


The Project Management tool is based on previous research that was conducted in the summer of 2018 to the summer of 2019. We wanted to find a way to incorporate cyber-security into the software development life-cycle. We researched many existing models from Waterfall models to Agile models. We expanded our research into Behavioral Driven development, SAFe, DevOps and DevOps and DevSecOps We decived to focus more on Agile developent and focused more on DevSecOps. We cmpared simmilar DevSecOps models and developed a criteria for a better and more efficient model. The mpdel is virtually incoorporated into this tool for users to follow.
